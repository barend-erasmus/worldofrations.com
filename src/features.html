Nutrient Composition

With the Nutrient Composition, we can view the value of each nutrient along with it's status. This status will indicate whether the nutrient is within the boundaries of the ideal ration. The macronutrients will always be adequate as they are taken into account upon formulation where the micronutrients is not. The inadequate micronutrients will be used to formulate a supplement mix which can we seen on the next tab.
