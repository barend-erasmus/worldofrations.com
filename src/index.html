Who are we

Finding the optimal ration according to the feedstuffs available to you.

World of Rations® is an online cloud-based ration formulation program, structured in such a way as to suit the farmer, specialist nutritionist, feed milling companies and academics alike. The program formulates rations in a true least-cost linear programming procedure. The user merely has to select from the extensive list of feedstuffs the feeds available to him and submit the cost of the selected ingredients.

Our Features

Our least-cost ration formulator offers its users any one of two options.

Learn More
Micronutrients excluded

The 'Micronutrients excluded' option formulates a least-cost ration in two stages. It first formulates a ration to its macronutrient stage and supplies thereafter a supplement micronutrient mix which, when added to the feed, will rectify all deficiencies found in the initial macronutrient formulation.

Micronutrients included

The 'Micronutrients included' option is meant for those who know exactly which or what feedstuffs are necessary to arrive at a successful solution and is ideal for professionals.
