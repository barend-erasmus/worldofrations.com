About

Bringing tech closer to the farmers, nutritionists and feed milling companies.

World of Rations is an agricultural tech company with their focus on find the optimal ration dictated by the available feedstuffs. Founded in Cape Town by two people passionate about their industry.

Dr. Savas Couvaras is a well-known and respected nutritionist in the Western Cape who spent several years at EPol.

Barend Erasmus is a technology enthusiast with over 5 years experience in both corporate and start-up like environments.

Learn More
Micronutrients excluded

The 'Micronutrients excluded' option formulates a least-cost ration in two stages. It first formulates a ration to its macronutrient stage and supplies thereafter a supplement micronutrient mix which, when added to the feed, will rectify all deficiencies found in the initial macronutrient formulation.

Micronutrients included

The 'Micronutrients included' option is meant for those who know exactly which or what feedstuffs are necessary to arrive at a successful solution and is ideal for professionals.
