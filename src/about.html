Who are we?

World of Rations is an agricultural tech company with their focus on finding the optimal ration dictated by the available feedstuffs. Founded in Cape Town by two people passionate about their industry.

Dr. Savas Couvaras is a well-known and respected nutritionist in the Western Cape who spent several years at EPol.

Barend Erasmus is a technology enthusiast with over 5 years experience in both corporate and start-up like environments.

World of Rations is looking to incorporate many more features. Feel free to send us suggestions via email.
